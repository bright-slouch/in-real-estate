## Gift Ideas by Client Type

### First-Time Buyers
- Custom return address stamp with new address
- Framed watercolor of the new home (commission from local artist)
- Personalized doormat
- Smart home starter kit (Nest thermostat, Ring doorbell)
- Local restaurant gift card + "explore your new neighborhood" note
- Home maintenance starter kit (toolbox essentials)

### Move-Up / Experienced Buyers
- Upscale local experience (dinner at a notable restaurant, spa gift card)
- Custom house portrait (oil or watercolor)
- High-quality cutting board or kitchen item (personalized with address/names)
- Wine or spirits from a local Indiana vineyard or distillery
- Donation to a charity in client's name

### Sellers Only (Already Have a New Home)
- House-themed candleholder, art piece, or decorative item
- Gift card to local nursery or garden center
- Indiana-made goods basket (local honey, jam, candles)
- Restaurant experience at a place they mentioned loving

### Families with Children
- Custom family portrait commission
- "Our First Year" photo book template + gift card
- Local activity or museum membership

### Investors / Repeat Clients
- Premium bottle of wine or whiskey
- High-end business card holder or leather portfolio
- Local experience (golf round, concert tickets)
- Thoughtful note acknowledging the ongoing relationship
