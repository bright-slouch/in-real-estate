## Important Notes

- **Loan Estimate:** Your lender is required to provide an official Loan Estimate within 3 business days of loan application. That document controls over this estimate.
- **Rate lock:** This estimate is based on a rate of {interest_rate}%. Rates change daily — locking your rate with the lender eliminates rate change risk.
- **Wire fraud warning:** You will receive instructions for wiring closing funds. Verify wiring instructions directly with the title company by calling the number on their official website — never wire based on email instructions alone.
