---
description: Personalized real estate news briefing from IBJ, Google News, and Indiana feeds
argument-hint: "[optional: focus area or 'full briefing']"
---

Run the `real-estate-plugin:re-news-briefing` skill. Fetches IBJ and Google News RSS feeds, de-duplicates headlines, categorizes stories, and formats a scannable morning briefing tailored to the agent's market areas. Optionally generates a social media post from the top story.
