## The Buying Process: A-to-Z Overview

### Step 1: Get Pre-Approved (If You Haven't Already)

**Why this matters:** Pre-approval:
- Tells you exactly what you can afford
- Shows sellers you're a serious, qualified buyer
- Gives us negotiating power — sellers prefer pre-approved buyers
- Prevents heartbreak over a home you fall in love with but can't finance

**Lenders I trust:** I work with several excellent local lenders who are responsive, competitive, and know this market. I'm happy to introduce you to a few options — you're always free to choose your own lender.

### Step 2: Define Your Search

We'll work together to identify your:
- **Must-haves:** Non-negotiable requirements
- **Strong preferences:** Important but flexible
- **Nice-to-haves:** Would be great but not required
- **Deal-breakers:** What will end the search immediately

Factors to consider: price range, location (county/city/school district), size, bedrooms/bathrooms, lot size, age of home, style, commute distance, HOA preferences.

### Step 3: Active Search

I'll set you up with an automated search on the BLC (Indiana MLS) that sends you new listings as they hit the market. In this market, acting quickly on the right property is important.

**Search sources:**
- MIBOR BLC (MLS) — the authoritative source for all listed properties
- Pocket listings — off-market opportunities I hear about through my network
- For-sale-by-owner — I can represent you on FSBO properties too
- New construction — I'll register you with builders so you're my client (at no extra cost to you)

### Step 4: Showings

We'll tour properties together. Here's what to focus on:
- The neighborhood feel — can you picture yourself here?
- Layout and flow — does it work for how you actually live?
- Condition — what's cosmetic vs. what's a real issue?
- Bones of the house — roof age, HVAC age, windows, foundation
- The things you can't change (location, lot, neighbors, noise)

I'll share my honest observations. My job isn't to talk you into anything.

### Step 5: Making an Offer

When you find the right home, we'll act quickly. I'll:
- Analyze recent comparable sales to establish a fair value
- Review the seller's disclosure form for any red flags
- Recommend an offer price and terms strategy
- Explain every line of the Purchase Agreement before you sign
- Present your offer professionally to give it the best chance of acceptance

### Step 6: Under Contract — Due Diligence Period

Once accepted, you have approximately **10 days** for inspections. During this time:
- Home inspection (general + any specialists I recommend based on the property)
- Review inspection findings together
- Decide if you want to request repairs, credits, or walk away
- Your earnest money is at risk after this period if you back out without contingency basis

### Step 7: Financing and Appraisal

Your lender will order an appraisal and process your loan. My job:
- Keep in close contact with your lender to track progress
- Flag any delays early
- Help navigate appraisal issues if the property comes in below contract price

### Step 8: Clear to Close and Closing

When your lender says "clear to close," we're almost there:
- Final walkthrough (24-48 hours before closing)
- Closing at the title company
- Sign paperwork, fund your loan, get your keys
