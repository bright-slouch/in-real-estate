# Central Indiana School Districts Reference

> Used by `re-neighborhood-research` to identify district assignments, school names, and enrollment contacts.
> Ratings noted as "directional only" — see caveat below. Always direct buyers to verify enrollment eligibility at the district level.

---

## CRITICAL: City Name ≠ School District

School district boundaries in Central Indiana do not follow municipal boundaries. A home in the "City of Carmel" may be in Carmel Clay Schools, Hamilton Southeastern Schools, or Westfield-Washington Schools. A property in unincorporated Johnson County near Bargersville may be in Center Grove or Greenwood Community Schools. **Always verify via the district's address-based enrollment lookup or county assessor records before representing a school district to a buyer.**

---

## Rating Caveat (Required)

GreatSchools ratings referenced below are **directional only**. They reflect a point-in-time snapshot of academic performance metrics and should not be treated as definitive quality judgments. Ratings shift as demographics, funding, and leadership change. They do not capture school culture, extracurricular strength, special education quality, or fit for a specific child. Direct buyers to tour schools and contact enrollment offices directly.

**Do not use school ratings as a proxy for neighborhood demographics. This is a Fair Housing violation.**

## Highest-Demand Districts: Why Buyers Pay the Premium

**Carmel Clay Schools** — Historically the top academic reputation in the state. Carmel High School draws national attention for academics and performing arts. The brand alone drives 15-25% premiums. Buyers relocating from high-performing suburbs in Chicago, Columbus, or Columbus-Ohio specifically request it.

**Hamilton Southeastern (HSE)** — The growth story. Fishers has been one of the fastest-growing cities in the US. HSE tracks that growth with newer facilities and a strong track record. Often second choice for buyers priced out of Carmel or wanting newer construction.

**Zionsville Community Schools** — The small-town luxury alternative. Buyers who want top schools without the scale of Carmel choose Zionsville. The Boone County location means lower tax rates than Hamilton County equivalents.

**Center Grove** — The Johnson County outlier. In a county better known for affordability, Center Grove commands a real premium. Buyers who discover it often say it was the deciding factor in choosing Johnson County over Hamilton.

## How to Use This Reference

1. Get the property address or zip code from the buyer or listing.
2. Look up the correct district using the district's address lookup tool — do not guess from city name.
3. Pull the high school and relevant feeder schools for that specific address.
4. Include the GreatSchools directional note and district contact info in the buyer report.
5. Suggest the buyer visit the district's website and call the enrollment office for the most current information.
